My Review of Call of Duty Black Ops II

My absolute favorite game of this series is Black Ops II. In my opinion it is hands down the best entry.
Everything was at a near perfect level starting with the campaign which throws twists and turns at you.
Its emotional and the best part in my opinion is how there are multiple endings so each playthrough could
be entirely different or completly the same. The next part of this amazing game is the amazing multiplayer.
It was balanced and yet very fast paced. There was a giant selection of weapons and abilities. The last and
my personal favorite gamemode is the thrilling and addicting zombies mode. The gist of the mode is that you
(solo or with 3 friends) have to survive a horde of zombies that are trying to eat you, it may seem simple but
there are many more things that make it interesting. from special perks to upgrading your weapons. My favorite
part is that there is a special easter egg on each map that uncovers a deep storyline that is continued from map
to map. With these three amazing elements it makes Black Ops II my favorite game of all time.

My Review of Pokemon Sapphire

My second favorite game of all time would have to be pokemon sapphire. It was my first pokemon game and my
favorite entry in the franchise. Pokemon is about collecting monsters and having them battle for you. You battle
other trainers and try to beat them. There are 8 gym leaders in this game, after you defeat each one you go on to face
the elite four and eventually the champion of the reigon you are in. Pokemon sapphire takes place in the Hoen reigon
which is shaped a bit after hawai. Along the way you encounter an evil team of villians thhat you have to defeat in order
to save the reigon and the world. The most appealing thing to me about this game is how even after you beat the champion
there is still so much to do. You can complete your pokedex by catching all 315 pokemon and even capture some legendary
pokemon. You can battle online and trade with others. I have seen people sink in hundreds of hours. You could go years without
completing the entire game %100. I personaly have sank many hours and the addicting nature and appealing charm of the game
still draws my towards it to this day. This is why pokemon sapphire is my 2nd favorite game.
