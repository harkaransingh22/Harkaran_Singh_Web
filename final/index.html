About:

Rockstar Games is a video game publisher and developer that was founded in 1998 as a subsidiary of Take-Two Interactive. The two founders of the company were named Sam and Dan Houser. Rockstar Games publishes and developes Third-Person,Action-Adevnture games. Many of their games have very famous franchises such as Grand Theft Auto, Manhunt, Red Dead Redemption and Max Payne. Rockstar is often under fire for the amount of violence they incorporate into their games and the amount of freedom they give the player. Rockstar has been taken to court many times by people disliking what they include in their games. Many People view Rockstar as just a developer that enjoys only violence and a developer that can never have deeper messages in their games. However that is a false fact many Rockstar Games titles are misunderstood because of the violence they have but people tend to overlook the darker themes that Rockstar includes. Some of these themes include loss, revenge, betrayal, drugs, government corruption and torture. Many of the more darker themes are overlooked due to the fun and wacky nature they use to cloud the serious tone. The new trend in the gaming industry is releasing sequels to games that dont need them just to cash in on the success of the original Game. Rockstar never releases an unfinished game they spend years upon years perfecting the game to make sure that consumers get their moneys worth. The reasons listed are just touching the tip of the iceberg on why Rockstar Games in my opinion is the best developer in the video game industry.


Subsidiaries

There are many different versions of Rockstar Games that are called subsidiaries. Each subsidiary has their own staff and heads. They are treated as their own company rather than just a part of the main company. The names of the current Rockstar games subsidiaries are; RAGE Technology Group, Rockstar India, Rockstar International, Rockstar Leeds, Lockstar Lincoln, Rockstar London, Rockstar New England, Rockstar North, Rockstar San Diego and Rockstar Toronto. Rockstar International is the main publishing headquarters for Rockstar Games but they do not make any games. The two most important subsidiaries out of all of them are Rockstar North and, Rockstar San Diego.

Rockstar North is a subsidiary of Rockstar Games and is located Edinburgh, Scotland and works on the Grand Theft Auto and Manhunt Franchises. The company was founded as Acme Software, in Dundee in 1984, by classmates David Jones, Russell Kay, Steve Hammond, and Mike Dailly, and was renamed DMA Design in 1987. As DMA Design they released popular games such as menace and blood money. In 1997 DMA released Grand Theft Auto and the game was met with immense success. DMA was later acquired by Take-Two Interactive and renamed Rockstar North.

Rockstar San Diego is located in Carlsbad, California and works on the Red Dead Redemption, Midnight Club and Smuglers Run franchises as well as helping on Grand Theft Auto and Max Payne. Orginaly Known as Angel Studios Inc, the company was founded by Colombian artist Diego Angel in 1984. The company started out working in the video game industry in the mid-1990s. The company released Midtown Madness, Midtown Madness 2 as well as an N64 port of Resident Evil 2. They Company was later acquired by Take-Two Interactive in 2002.
